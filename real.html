Benefits to Property Owners/Managers

Attract & Retain Residents

Stand out by offering something unique: a ‘community car’ that residents can use. Great for urban renters who don’t own cars – they’ll choose your building for the occasional mobility it provides.

Turnkey Service

Fore handles everything – vehicle provision, insurance, bookings, cleaning, maintenance. This is a zero-hassle amenity for you. You simply allow us to use a parking space and promote it to residents.

Improve Parking Efficiency

Encourage a car-sharing mindset. Some residents may choose not to bring a second car (or any car) if they know a shared one is available, freeing up parking space and reducing congestion.

Revenue Sharing (Optional)

Create a new revenue stream. If desired, we can share usage revenue with the property or set up a model where the property subsidizes a car for free use up to X hours per resident, with additional usage paid – flexible arrangements to suit your goals.

Modern & Eco-Friendly Image

Offering an on-site EV or hybrid for residents positions your property as eco-conscious and tech-savvy. It can support green building certifications and appeal to those who care about sustainability.

Customized Integration

Our system can integrate with your existing resident app or keycard system. Only authorized residents will be able to access the vehicles. We can also provide co-branded materials so it feels like a seamless part of your amenities.

Safety and Reliability

Vehicles are insured and covered by Fore. We ensure they are regularly cleaned and well-maintained. Residents reserve through the app, so there is a digital record of usage. We can even set usage rules (like no smoking, must refuel if under 1/4 tank) to keep things orderly.

How It Works for the Property

01

Initial Setup

You reach out to Fore and we assess your property’s needs (how many units, interest in a car, suitable parking spot, etc.). We’ll recommend the number and type of vehicles. Typically, one vehicle can serve many residents (we’ll use usage data to optimize).

02

Agreement

We sign an agreement covering the placement of our vehicle(s) at your site and the service terms. Decide on any cost-sharing or subsidy model. Fore can either charge residents directly per use, or you can cover some cost as an included amenity – we’re flexible.

03

Launch

Fore delivers the vehicle(s) and sets up signage and instructions. We hold a demo event or provide materials so residents know how to sign up and use the service. We can co-brand the experience (e.g., call it ‘[Building Name] Car Share, powered by Fore’).

04

Operation

Residents use the car via the Fore app. They reserve time slots and unlock the car with their phone. The car might have a home parking spot with a sign (which we’ll provide). Fore monitors and manages usage. We handle all cleaning, maintenance, and can swap out the car if needed for servicing.

05

Support

If a resident has an issue while using the car, they contact Fore’s support (24/7). We take care of it just as we would with any subscriber. Property management isn’t burdened with these day-to-day issues.

06

Ongoing

We provide the property with periodic usage reports – e.g., ‘The car was used X times this month by Y different residents, total mileage, etc.’ You can see the value it’s providing. If demand is consistently high (fully booked car), we might suggest adding another vehicle. If it’s low, we could adjust availability or remove if needed (with notice) – so you’re never stuck.

Unlock

Use your phone to instantly unlock the car, right from your building entrance. No keys, no waiting — just tap and go.

Return

Park the car back in the same spot when you’re
done. No searching for spaces, no extra charges.
